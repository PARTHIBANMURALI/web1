AUDITORIUM

Our school hosts an exhilarating Annual Sports Day, where students from all grades come together to showcase their athletic talents and team spirit. The day is filled with various track and field events, including sprints, relay races, long jumps, and high jumps. Students compete in a friendly and competitive atmosphere, cheered on by their peers and teachers. The event aims to promote physical fitness, sportsmanship, and healthy competition among students.
